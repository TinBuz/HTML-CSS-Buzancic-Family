Lazer Academy is is an Electronic Dance Music production group founded in 2012 by Tin Buzancic and Eugene Choe. The group consists of Tin Buzancic and Eugene Choe with co-producer/manager Fedja Buzancic. After the release of their hot single Drop It Like, the trio realized their potenetial because of all the positive feeback. With music ranging from dubstep and electro house to moombahton and glitch hop, this is only the beginning for the young group.
